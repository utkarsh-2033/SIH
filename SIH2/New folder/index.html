About RSTES

Rajathan Government has constituted Rajathan State Technical Education Society in the Department of State Technical Education, for the implementation of transparent and uniform procedure for admissions to various graduate and diploma courses like B.Tech , B.Pharma B.Arch , Diploma in different disciplines of Engineering, Technology, Architecture, and more.
